The Invisible Sword

A Forgotten Soul. A Hidden Legacy. A War Reborn.

About the Book

The Invisible Sword is a gripping tale of destiny, courage, and ancient power. When a seemingly ordinary college student stumbles upon a mysterious glowing blade, he is thrust into a world of forgotten legends and dark forces that have long been buried in time. Haunted by visions of a past life and pursued by an ancient evil, he must uncover the truth about his identity and embrace his role in a war that never truly ended.

About the Author

Vishnu Siripuram is a passionate storyteller who blends mythology, fantasy, and emotion to create immersive fictional worlds. Through his writing, he brings forgotten legends to life for modern readers.
